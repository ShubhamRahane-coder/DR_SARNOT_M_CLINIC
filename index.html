Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Root Canal Treatment painful at Sarnot Clinic? expand_more
We use advanced local anesthesia and computerized rotary systems that make the RCT procedure virtually painless and often completed in a single visit.
How long do dental implants last? expand_more
With proper care and maintenance, dental implants are designed to be a permanent solution and can last a lifetime. Regular checkups and good oral hygiene are essential.
Do you provide treatments for hair loss? expand_more
Yes, we offer advanced Hair PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy and other medical treatments for hair thinning and hair loss for both men and women.
