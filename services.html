Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our clinical services and procedures.

Do you offer pain-free treatments?

expand_more
Yes, we prioritize patient comfort and use advanced local anesthesia techniques and computerized rotary systems to ensure your experience is as comfortable and pain-free as possible.

How long does a smile makeover take?

expand_more
A smile makeover timeline varies based on your individual needs. Simple cases can take 2-3 visits, while more complex transformations involving implants or orthodontics may take several months. We will provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Are laser treatments safe for all skin types?

expand_more
We use state-of-the-art laser technology that is safe and effective for various skin types. Our specialists will perform a thorough skin analysis before any treatment to ensure the most appropriate and safest approach for your specific skin profile.
