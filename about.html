About SARNOT Clinic

Complete dental and cosmetic solutions with 14+ years of trusted care

A Message from Our Founder

We believe a healthy smile is more than just good oral hygiene—it's a reflection of confidence and well-being. Since 2010, our mission has been to offer accessible, personalized dental care that blends clinical expertise with a human touch.

We aim to make every visit comfortable, every treatment effective, and every patient feel like family. Our clinic is built on care, trust, and patient-first principles.

Thank you for trusting us with your smile.

Dr. Mahavir Sarnot, MDS
Founder & Chief Dentist | Endodontist & Implantologist

A Message from Our Co-Founder

Beauty and health go hand in hand. My passion lies in helping our patients feel confident in their smiles through advanced cosmetic and skin treatments. Combined with Dr. Mahavir's expertise in restorative dentistry, we create comprehensive solutions that enhance both function and aesthetics.

Every patient is unique, and we take time to understand your individual goals and concerns. Whether it's cosmetic dentistry, skin rejuvenation, or hair restoration, our approach is always patient-centric and results-driven.

I'm honored to be part of your transformation journey.

Dr. Priyanka Sarnot, BDS
Co-Founder & Specialist | Cosmetic Dentistry & Skin Treatment
